# 👥 Customer Segmentation Dashboard (RFM Analysis + K-Means Clustering)

An interactive **Streamlit dashboard** developed to segment customers using **RFM Analysis** and **K-Means Clustering**. The project helps businesses understand customer behavior, identify valuable customer groups, and support data-driven marketing strategies.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer segmentation is an essential business strategy for understanding customer purchasing behavior and improving marketing effectiveness. This project applies **RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) Analysis** combined with **K-Means Clustering** to classify customers into meaningful segments. The results are presented through an interactive Streamlit dashboard for easy exploration and decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Many businesses collect large amounts of customer transaction data but do not fully utilize it to understand customer behavior. As a result:

- Marketing campaigns are less targeted.
- High-value customers are difficult to identify.
- Customer retention strategies become less effective.
- Business opportunities from customer insights are often overlooked.

---

## 💡 Solution

A customer segmentation dashboard was developed using **Python** and **Streamlit**.

The project includes:

- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) calculation
- Customer segmentation using K-Means Clustering
- Interactive dashboard development with Streamlit
- Online deployment for easy access

## 📈 Results

The project successfully identified **four customer segments**:

- ⭐ High-Value / VIP Customers
- 💎 Loyal Mid-Value Customers
- 🛍️ Occasional Low-Value Customers
- ⚠️ Lost / Dormant Customers

The interactive dashboard enables users to monitor customer segmentation and explore business insights through an intuitive web application.
